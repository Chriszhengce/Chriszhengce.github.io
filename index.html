Welcome to my homepage. I am currently a third year phD candidate major in Electrical Engineering, and a research assistant in the Digital Television and Broadcasting Laboratory. I spent a wonderful semester as an undergraduate exchange student in the Technische Universitaet Muenchen from Sep 2015 to Feb 2016, and two wonderful months from Jan-March 2019 as a visiting student in Telecom ParisTech.

My Research Interests includes age-of-information optimization and stochastic network optimization. Previously, I have worked on modulation techniques in wireless communication systems.
