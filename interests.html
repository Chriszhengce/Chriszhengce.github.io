Haoyue Tang – Research Interests

Age of Information

Data freshness is important for real-time systems such real-time monitoring, positioning and automatic control systems. The "Age-of-Information" measures the time elapsed since the generation time-stamp of the freshest information at the receiver. Unlike previous quality of service (QoS) metrics such as throughput and delay, AoI measures data freshness from the receiver perspective. To achieve a small AoI performance, packets with short delay have to be transmitted in a timely manner.

I'm currently working on scheduling and routing to minimize AoI performance in wireless networks. See my publications for more details.

Previous Focus: Spatial Modulation MIMO systems

Massive Multiple Input and Multiple Output (MIMO) system has been proved as a key technology in 5G system. It makes a clean break with current practice through the use of a very large number of service antennas that are operated fully coherently and adaptively. Spatial Modulation system has been proposed to overcome the challenge of energy comsumptions. Capacity Analysis, Energy Efficiency Analysis are needed for thorough understanding of the system. Precise Channel Estimation is also needed for signal detection.
