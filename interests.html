Haoyue Tang – Research Interests

Information Theoretic Security

A system is considered perfect security, if the evasdropper is assumed to possess unlimited computation resourses. To achieve this, physical layer encryption is proposed by Wyner, which is provable, unbreakable, and quantifiable. Aided with mutiple antenna system and network information theory, theoretic analysis of the proposed system recently calls for more attention.

Previous Focus: Spatial Modulation MIMO systems

Massive Multiple Input and Multiple Output (MIMO) system has been proved as a key technology in 5G system. It makes a clean break with current practice through the use of a very large number of service antennas that are operated fully coherently and adaptively. Spatial Modulation system has been proposed to overcome the challenge of energy comsumptions. Capacity Analysis, Energy Efficiency Analysis are needed for thorough understanding of the system. Precise Channel Estimation is also needed for signal detection.
