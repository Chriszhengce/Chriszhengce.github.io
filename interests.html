Haoyue Tang – Research Interests

Massive MIMO

Massive Multiple Input and Multiple Output (MIMO) system has been proved as a key technology in 5G system. It makes a clean break with current practice through the use of a very large number of service antennas that are operated fully coherently and adaptively. Spatial Modulation and Low Resolution ADC/DACs MIMO system have been proposed to overcome the challenge of energy comsumptions. Capacity Analysis, Energy Efficiency Analysis are needed for thorough understanding of the system. Precise Channel Estimation is also needed for signal detection.

My current Research Focus includes:

  • Massive MIMO with low Resolution ADC/DACs, Capacity Analysis and Channel Estimation

  • Milimeterwave Massive MIMO
